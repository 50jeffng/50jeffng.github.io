About


Studying Computer Science, I always fancy solving problems using code.

Spending most of my childhood playing games on my family's shared computer, every game gave me the opportunity to become something I wasn't. A new world, with any character I can be, going on an adventure. I wanted to make these, games that I could share to my friends. I also liked playing simulation & strategy games particularly, even though I'm not the sharpest tool in the shed, I always liked to solve problems.

On my last year in my high school, I had to choose an elective class. I chose computing class, which taught me programming, and I never looked back. When I first coded in Visual Basic, I realised I could do possibly anything by just typing out code to a computer.

Then from someone who had no direction in what to do after secondary education, I was pumped with enthusiasm.

And I embarked upon a new path of becoming a Computer Scientist.
